# Resonant Minds: Closed-Loop Social Avatars with Theory of Mind

Resonant Minds studies how digital humans can engage in genuine social interactions through a closed-loop framework integrating perception, social reasoning (Theory of Mind), and multimodal expression generation. Our system generates emotionally-controllable dual-agent videos with synchronized speech, capturing bidirectional conversational dynamics that prior single-character talking-head methods cannot produce.

We will open-source code, data, and benchmark. Stay tuned!

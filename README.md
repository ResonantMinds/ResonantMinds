## Overview

**Resonant Minds** studies how digital humans can engage in genuine social interactions through a closed-loop dual-agent framework. Our system unifies **perception**, **social reasoning (Theory of Mind)**, and **multimodal expression generation** into a single coherent interaction cycle, generating emotionally-controllable dual-agent videos with synchronized speech. Unlike prior single-character talking-head methods, we capture bidirectional conversational dynamics between socially-aware agents with private goals and distinct personas.
